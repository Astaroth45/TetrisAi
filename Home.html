This project was carried out in the school environment with the educational purpose. So feel free to modify and improve.
One more thing, if you want to change the controls feel free to do so, they are in the "game_manager_player" part.
The default controls are:

Space Bar to drop piece
Z Key to rotate piece
Down Arrow to move 1 square down
Right Key to move 1 square to the right
Left Key to move 1 square to the left
